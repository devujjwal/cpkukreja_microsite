About C.P. Kukreja Architects

redefine architecture and planning in India, CP Kukreja Architects (CPKA) is now one of the most influential architectural practices of the country. With a highly qualified and professional team of architects, urban planners, structural engineers, and surveyors, CPKA is a multi-disciplinary firm known for some of the most iconic buildings across the globe and our commitment to design excellence, innovation, and sustainability.

CPKA has been selected by one of the world’s leading architectural journals, World Architecture, U.K. among the top 100 architecture firms in the world. It was also touted as the “Best Multi-disciplinary Architecture and Engineering Firm” by Build News, London, UK in 2016. CNBC and CRISIL selected CPKA for “Best Architects in India in the design of Residential Buildings”.
